## Prompt

Design a full-width hero section with:
- Animated gradient background (slow color shift)
- Large headline with gradient text
- Subtitle with muted secondary color
- Two CTA buttons: primary (filled) and secondary (outline)
- Responsive: stacks vertically on mobile
- Dark mode first design

## Notes

- Use `background-size: 400%` with `@keyframes` for smooth gradient animation.
- Keep headline under 8 words for impact.
